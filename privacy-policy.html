1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

We collect information you voluntarily provide when you interact with our website:

Activity Data Collected Purpose
Placing an Order Full name, phone number, email, complete shipping address, product details, quantity, color preference Order fulfillment & delivery
Payment Verification Transaction ID, payment amount, screenshot (optional) Confirm payment received
Account Registration Email address, encrypted password Order tracking & reviews
Product Reviews Display name, rating, review text, purchase verification Build product credibility
Customer Support Name, contact details, message content Resolve queries

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

When you browse our website, we automatically collect certain technical data:

  • Device Information: IP address, browser type & version, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, click patterns, referral source
  • Location Data: General geographic location derived from IP address (city/province level only)

Important: We do NOT collect sensitive data like bank account numbers, credit card details, CNIC, or passwords in plain text. Payment screenshots are deleted after verification.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information strictly for legitimate business purposes:

  1. Order Processing: To confirm, pack, ship your orders and provide tracking updates
  2. Payment Verification: To match your payment with your order and prevent fraud
  3. Customer Communication: To send order confirmations, shipping notifications, and respond to inquiries via WhatsApp/email
  4. Account Services: To maintain your account, enable order history access, and authenticate reviews
  5. Service Improvement: To analyze website traffic, identify popular products, and fix technical issues
  6. Legal Compliance: To comply with tax laws, consumer protection regulations, and court orders if required
  7. Fraud Prevention: To detect and prevent fraudulent transactions or account misuse

3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to any third party for marketing purposes. Limited sharing occurs only in these cases:

3.1 Service Providers

  • Courier Partners: TCS, Leopards, Call Courier receive your name, phone, and address solely for delivery
  • Payment Gateways: Easypaisa/JazzCash process transaction verification only
  • Cloud Infrastructure: Google Firebase stores encrypted account data and order information

3.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by Pakistani law, court order, or to protect our rights, property, or safety, or that of our customers.

3.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, customer information may be transferred. We will notify you via email and website notice before your data is transferred.

4. Data Security Measures

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmission uses SSL/TLS 256-bit encryption. Passwords are hashed using Firebase Authentication
  • Access Control: Only authorized staff with confidentiality agreements can access customer data
  • Secure Storage: Databases are hosted on Google Firebase with enterprise-grade security
  • Regular Audits: We conduct periodic security reviews and update systems
  • Payment Security: We never store card details. Screenshots are deleted within 30 days of order completion
Security Notice: While we use best practices, no internet transmission is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and do not share account credentials.

5. Data Retention Policy

6. Your Privacy Rights

Under applicable data protection principles, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information in your account
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your account and associated personal data
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Ask us to limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object: Opt-out of marketing communications or certain data processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Unsubscribe or delete account anytime

To exercise any right, contact us at haidertraders@gmail.com or WhatsApp +92 347 1881297. We respond within 7 business days.

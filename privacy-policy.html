Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 19, 2026

Welcome to Haider Commercial Traders. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase from us.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the site.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us when you:

  • Place an Order: Name, phone number, email address, shipping address, product details, quantity, and color preferences.
  • Make Payment: Transaction ID, payment amount, and payment screenshot (optional). We do not collect or store your bank account details, credit card information, or passwords.
  • Create an Account: Email address and password (encrypted) for order tracking and reviews.
  • Submit Reviews: Name, review content, and rating.
  • Contact Us: Name, email, phone number, and message content via WhatsApp or contact forms.

1.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you access our website, we may automatically collect certain information about your device, including:

  • IP address, browser type, and operating system
  • Pages viewed, time spent on pages, and referring URLs
  • Device information and mobile network information

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Order Processing: To process your orders, arrange delivery, and provide customer support.
  • Payment Verification: To verify your payment and confirm your order status.
  • Communication: To send order confirmations, shipping updates, and respond to your inquiries via WhatsApp, email, or phone.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, enable you to track orders and submit reviews.
  • Service Improvement: To understand how users interact with our website and improve our products and services.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

3. How We Share Your Information

We respect your privacy. We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following situations:

  • Delivery Partners: We share your name, phone number, and address with courier services (TCS, Leopards, etc.) solely for delivering your order.
  • Payment Processors: Transaction details are shared with Easypaisa/JazzCash for payment verification purposes only.
  • Service Providers: We use Firebase (Google) for authentication and database services. Firebase may process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental regulation.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology
  • Passwords are encrypted and stored securely via Firebase Authentication
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only
  • Regular security assessments and updates are performed

However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your personal information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy:

  • Order Information: Retained for 5 years for accounting, warranty, and legal compliance
  • Account Information: Retained until you request account deletion
  • Reviews: Retained indefinitely to maintain product credibility, but can be removed upon request

6. Your Privacy Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction: You can request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion: You can request deletion of your account and personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.

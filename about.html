Our Story

Haider Commercial Traders was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make premium home appliances and furniture accessible to every household in Pakistan. Based in Dharma, Depalpur, Punjab, we started as a small local business with a big dream.

What began as a passion project has grown into one of the region's most trusted online stores for home essentials. We believe that quality home appliances shouldn't be a luxury—they should be available to everyone at fair prices with honest service.

2020

The Beginning

Started with a small showroom in Depalpur, focusing on quality over quantity. Our first 100 customers became our brand ambassadors.

2022

Going Digital

Launched our online platform to serve customers across Punjab. Introduced WhatsApp ordering for customer convenience.

2024

Expansion

Expanded product range to 500+ items. Partnered with top courier services for nationwide delivery.

2026

Today

Serving 5000+ satisfied customers with 4.8★ rating. Continuing to grow while maintaining our commitment to quality and service.
